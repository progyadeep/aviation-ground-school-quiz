Download GROUND THEORY QUIZ App



Latest version: 1.7.4, released on 22nd January 2020.

GROUND THEORY QUIZ is a free mobile app available (currently) for Android that helps you keep your ground theory knowledge of the following subjects updated:

  1. Air Navigation
  2. Aviation Meteorology
  3. Air Regulations
  4. Technical General

How it works: This app presents random questions of a particular topic or from all topics (as selected by the user) along with three options to choose the answer from. You know how this works.

NOTE: This app requires an active internet connection to work as it fetches questions from an online server.
